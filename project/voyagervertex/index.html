VoyagerVertex

VoyagerVertex, the "Traveler's Touchstone", is an all-in-one travel planning tool tailored for modern explorers. By integrating AI with crowd-sourced data, it offers an intuitive platform for travelers to craft, refine, and experience journeys that align with their unique tastes. With personalized recommendations and genuine reviews, users can dive deep into authentic local experiences without the usual travel hassles.

This is a project for UofT SCS UX/UI Design bootcamp.
