Healthcare Hub

Healthcare Hub is a comprehensive digital platform that connects users to medical professionals, enables easy medication refills, and offers real-time consultation, making healthcare more accessible and personalized.

This is a project for UofT SCS UX/UI Design bootcamp.

However, due to the project's time constraints, our journey with the healthcare Hub has currenly culminated in a mid-fidelity prototype.
