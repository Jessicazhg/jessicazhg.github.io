HarmonyHub

HarmonyHub revolutionizes the live music scene, connecting venue owners, artists, and concert-goers. Artists showcase their work to attrack bookings, while owners manage events seamlessly. Attendees explore and buy tickets for shows, creating an enriched, harmonious ecosystem for the live music industry.

This is a project for Coursera Google UX Design Professional Certificate.
